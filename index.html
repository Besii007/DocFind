    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I find a doctor?

    You can find a doctor by using our search feature. Filter by specialty, location, or insurance accepted. Each doctor profile includes credentials, patient reviews, and available appointment times.

    Is there a consultation fee?

    DocFind does not charge a fee for booking appointments. However, standard medical consultation fees apply and vary by doctor. Many insurance plans are accepted, and fee information is available on each doctor's profile.

    How far in advance should I book?

    We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for routine appointments. For urgent matters, many doctors offer same-day or next-day appointments. Emergency situations should always be directed to your nearest emergency room.

    What is the cancellation policy?

    Appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without penalty. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee, which varies by practice.
