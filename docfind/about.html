    Our Story

    Founded in 2018, DocFind was born out of a simple yet powerful idea: healthcare should be accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our founder, Dr. Sarah Mitchell, experienced firsthand the challenges patients face when trying to find the right medical professional quickly.

    What started as a small directory of trusted doctors in New York has grown into a comprehensive platform connecting thousands of patients with certified healthcare professionals across the country every day.

    Today, DocFind continues to innovate and expand, guided by our core belief that quality healthcare should be just a few clicks away.

    Our Mission

    At DocFind, our mission is to transform how people access healthcare by creating the most trusted and convenient platform for finding and connecting with healthcare professionals.

    We believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare without the hassle. By streamlining the process of finding and booking appointments with verified doctors, we're removing barriers and making healthcare more accessible.

    We're committed to maintaining the highest standards of trust, reliability, and patient satisfaction in everything we do.
